

### **Answer Key**

**1. $x^2 - 6x + 5 = 0$**

* Move constant: $x^2 - 6x = -5$
* Add $\left(\frac{-6}{2}\right)^2 = 9$ to both sides: $x^2 - 6x + 9 = 4$
* Rewrite as binomial: $(x - 3)^2 = 4$
* Take the square root: $x - 3 = \pm 2$
* **Answers: $x = 5, x = 1$**
